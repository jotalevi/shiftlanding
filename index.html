Frequently Asked Questions

What is ERP migration, and why is it important?

ERP migration is the process of moving your business data from one ERP system to another. This is crucial for leveraging modern functionalities, improving efficiency, and staying adaptable to your business’s changing needs.

Which ERPs do you support for migration?

We offer migrations from any ERP system to Salesforce and Microsoft Dynamics. Our team ensures a smooth transition tailored to your business’s unique needs.

How long does the migration process take?

The migration time depends on your system’s complexity and data volume. Smaller projects can take weeks, while larger ones may take several months. We provide a detailed timeline after an initial consultation.

What are the costs associated with ERP migration?

Costs vary by project size and complexity. Basic migrations can start at $10,000, while more complex migrations may go up to $100,000 or more, depending on custom needs. Contact us for an exact estimate.

What measures do you take to ensure data security during migration?

We ensure data security through secure transfer protocols, data backups, and compliance with industry regulations. Our team conducts audits before and after migration to verify data integrity.

What support do you provide post-migration?

We offer comprehensive post-migration support, including training, troubleshooting, and ongoing technical assistance. The support period can range from 2 to 12 months depending on the selected plan.

Can I customize my new ERP system after migration?

Absolutely! Both Salesforce and Dynamics are highly customizable. After migration, we can help tailor the system to your specific business processes.

How do you handle data mapping during the migration?

We analyze your current data structure and create a detailed mapping strategy to ensure all critical data is accurately transferred and organized in the new system.
